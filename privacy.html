Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Word & Logic. We deeply respect your privacy and believe that your data belongs to you. This privacy policy outlines our simple approach to data collection: we do not collect any.

2. No Data Collection

We do not track, collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage metrics, or device identifiers to any external servers. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools within the application.

3. Local Storage Only

All of your app data—including your game progress, high scores, and personal settings—is stored entirely locally on your device. Because your data never leaves your device, you have complete privacy and control over it. Please note that uninstalling the app will permanently delete this local data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our app, you can contact us at: support@wordandlogic.example.com
